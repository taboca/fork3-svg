Who we Are?

Taboca is a small-business founded and directed by Marcio Galli and supported by a number of contributors from Brazil. Our contributors are hired based on the current projects we are running. This page may contain contributors that worked or participated in previous projects.

Marcio Galli — is the executive director at Taboca and responsible for management and development activities. Marcio coordinates the direction and strategy of our solution frameworks, open activities to support open-source projects, agreements and specification for customers, software development for client applications, user interface and user experience design.
